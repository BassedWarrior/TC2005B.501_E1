MECHANICS GAME STATISTICS

CHRONICLE DOOM

DESCRIPTION

Attrition card game, where the player's main goal is to hold back the waves of endless enemies who are bound to destroy the space-time continuum, and the player must spend their khronos resource to play unit and paradox cards, and hold out as long as they can with a deck that gets thinner every turn, until they eventually run out, and the player must make their last stand.

MINDSET

The player will feel overwhelmed by the attrition the constant waves imply for their limited amount of resources. They should be nervous and this will be achieved by making their units frail and outnumbered by the enemies, which will be a challenging experience for them to solve. We could consider adding different progression elements such as unlocking new cards or abilities to maintain long-term engagement and reward player progress.

how to play

Chronicle Doom.

The cycle will continue until your life goes down to ZERO.

resources

  • Player receives two cards from the deck if possible
  • Player adds +4 of Khronos
  • Enemies are deployed in the field and assigned for next rounds.

DRAG & DROP

  • Player moves unit or paradox cards to the map
  • Player seeks to balance his power to defend timelines
  • Previews are shown of the damage that will be done if the cards remain there.

CLASH TIME

  • The Paradox Cards effects are activated by assigning the attributes of the Paradox Cards
  • The damage received by the cards is calculated
  • The lifeless cards are eliminated
